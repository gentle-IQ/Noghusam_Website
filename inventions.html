Featured Invention

Noghusam Cocoa Pod
Breaking Machine

Engineered in Ghana. Built for Africa's farmers.

The Noghusam Cocoa Pod Breaking Machine is an indigenous agricultural innovation designed and fabricated entirely at Noghusam Tech Hub. Built to solve one of the most labour-intensive challenges in cocoa farming, this machine dramatically reduces the time and effort required to break open cocoa pods.

Developed through years of field research and iterative engineering, the machine is built from locally sourced materials using precision metal fabrication techniques, making it affordable, durable, and easy to maintain in rural farming communities.
