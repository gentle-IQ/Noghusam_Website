Who We Are

Built on Steel.
Driven by Craft.

Noghusam Tech Hub is a private metal fabrication and manufacturing company based in New Winneba, Ghana. Since 2012, we have been transforming raw metal into solutions that power farms, furnish offices, and inspire through art.

Our team of skilled fabrication professionals combines traditional craftsmanship with modern engineering techniques to deliver products that are durable, precise, and built to last.

From agricultural machinery that feeds communities to architectural sculptures that inspire, we are at the forefront of Ghana's fabrication industry.

Our Inventions

Featured Invention

Noghusam Cocoa Pod
Breaking Machine

Engineered in Ghana. Built for Africa's farmers.

The Noghusam Cocoa Pod Breaking Machine is an indigenous agricultural innovation designed and fabricated entirely at Noghusam Tech Hub. Built to solve one of the most labour-intensive challenges in cocoa farming, this machine dramatically reduces the time and effort required to break open cocoa pods — increasing productivity and reducing worker fatigue on farms across Ghana.

Developed through years of field research and iterative engineering, the machine is built from locally sourced materials using precision metal fabrication techniques, making it affordable, durable, and easy to maintain in rural farming communities.
